This greensheet details how to change your ½-stat.

\begin{enum}[lowering]
  \item Any time you stand within the ZOC of anything with a gold star  on it for more than 10 seconds, subtract 1 from your ½-score.
  \item Your ½-score cannot be lowered by an object with a gold star on it within one minute of the last time you were lowered by that same object.
  \item If a mempack or ability tells you to lower your ½-score, subtract 1 from your ½-score.

\end{enum}

The ½-stat cannot be raised, unless you know otherwise.
